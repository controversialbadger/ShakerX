## Special Pricing Notes

1. **Quantity Adjustments**:
   - For ShakerX products, orders must match specific box quantities
   - Orders will be adjusted to the nearest available quantity

2. **Color Considerations**:
   - Light colors on dark backgrounds may require underprinting for better visibility
   - Underprinting counts as an additional color (+0.50 EUR Net per piece)

3. **Volume Discounts**:
   - Special pricing available for very large orders
   - Pallet shipping offers more advantageous pricing structure
